Our Commitment to Your Privacy

SwipeSort is designed with privacy at its core. Your photos and personal data remain completely private and under your control.

The short version: We don't collect, access, transmit, or store any of your photos or personal data.

What We Don't Collect

  • Photos: Never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers
  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or phone numbers collected
  • Usage Data: No tracking of app usage or behavior
  • Device Information: No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Analytics: No analytics services or tracking

Photo Library Access

SwipeSort requests access to your photo library only to organize your photos:

  • All photo processing happens locally on your device
  • Your photos are never sent to external servers
  • We don't modify your original photos
  • You can control access anytime in iOS Settings > SwipeSort > Photos

Data Storage

SwipeSort stores minimal preferences locally on your device. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is never transmitted externally
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

If you have iCloud Photos enabled, your albums sync through Apple's iCloud service (controlled by Apple, not SwipeSort).

Third-Party Services

SwipeSort does not use any third-party services. No analytics, no advertising networks, no social media integrations, and no crash reporting services.

Children's Privacy

SwipeSort does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of SwipeSort after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
